About the Elf Adoption Agency


The Agency

The Elf Adoption Agency is committed to locating and re-homing any lost elves on the blockchain.
Over time we hope to expand on our services to cover other creatures, not just elves. We will strive to find as many homes as we can.

The Collection

This is a collection of cool elves that have been have been scattered and left homeless across the blockchain. Will you adopt one of these lonely elves and give them a new home?

This a collection of 5,000 elves that are waiting for adoption. We have some very special elves that we previously thought were extinct.

This is the inaugural collection of NFT's for JRthouse Media. This is a passion project as well as a project to develop and learn more about the evolving digital world we live in.
We hope to create a fresh and vibrant elf community as we go and hope you'll join us on this journey.
